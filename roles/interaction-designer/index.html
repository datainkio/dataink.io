Interaction Designer

As an interaction designer, my role is to craft intuitive and engaging user experiences that seamlessly connect people with digital products. I collaborate closely with cross-functional teams, including product managers, developers, and visual designers, to bring ideas to life. By prioritizing usability and accessibility, I ensure our products are accessible to everyone.

If you are looking for a human-experience designer, please do say hello – I look forward to hearing about your company and project.
