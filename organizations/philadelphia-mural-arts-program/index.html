Philadelphia Mural Arts Program: Transforming Communities through Art

The Philadelphia Mural Arts Program (Mural Arts) is the nation’s largest public art program, dedicated to transforming public spaces and communities through the power of art. Founded in 1984, Mural Arts has become a cornerstone of Philadelphia’s cultural landscape, fostering community engagement, social change, and artistic excellence through its vibrant and impactful murals.

Community Engagement

Mural Arts is deeply committed to involving the community in the creation of its murals. Through extensive community meetings, workshops, and collaborative painting sessions, residents actively participate in the artistic process, ensuring that the murals reflect their voices, stories, and aspirations.

Social Change

Mural Arts leverages the power of art to address social issues and promote positive change. The program’s initiatives tackle topics such as criminal justice reform, mental health, environmental sustainability, and youth empowerment. By raising awareness and sparking dialogue, Mural Arts fosters a sense of community and drives social action.

Artistic Excellence

With over 4,000 murals created across Philadelphia, Mural Arts showcases the work of both local and internationally renowned artists. The program’s commitment to high-quality, impactful art has earned it recognition as a leader in the public art movement, attracting visitors and art enthusiasts from around the world.

Educational Programs

Mural Arts offers a variety of educational programs designed to engage young people and adults in the arts. These programs include art education in schools, after-school programs, summer art camps, and professional development opportunities for emerging artists. By providing accessible art education, Mural Arts nurtures creativity and artistic skills in participants of all ages.

Public Art Tours

Mural Arts provides guided tours that explore the city’s vibrant mural landscape. These tours, available on foot, by trolley, or via bike, offer insights into the stories behind the murals, the artists who created them, and the communities they represent. The tours are a popular attraction, highlighting Philadelphia’s rich cultural heritage.

Sustainable Practices

Mural Arts is committed to sustainability and environmentally friendly practices. The program incorporates sustainable materials and methods into its mural creation process and focuses on themes of environmental stewardship in its artwork, promoting a message of ecological responsibility.

What Makes Philadelphia Mural Arts Program Stand Out?

Community-Centric Approach

Mural Arts’ dedication to community engagement ensures that each mural project reflects the voices and stories of local residents, fostering a sense of ownership and pride

Social Impact

The program’s focus on addressing social issues through art highlights its commitment to using creativity as a tool for positive change and social justice

Artistic Diversity and Excellence

With thousands of murals by a diverse range of artists, Mural Arts sets a high standard for public art, enriching Philadelphia’s urban landscape with beauty and meaning

Comprehensive Educational Programs

By offering extensive art education opportunities, Mural Arts nurtures the next generation of artists and instills a lifelong appreciation for the arts in participants

Cultural Tourism

The popular mural tours draw visitors from around the globe, showcasing Philadelphia’s unique cultural assets and boosting local tourism

Mural Arts’ commitment to sustainability and environmental themes in its artwork promotes ecological awareness and responsible practices
