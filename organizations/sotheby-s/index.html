Sotheby's: Defining Excellence in Art and Luxury

Sotheby’s is one of the world’s leading auction houses, renowned for its expertise in fine art, luxury items, and collectibles. Founded in 1744, Sotheby’s has a storied history and a reputation for excellence, serving discerning clients through high-profile auctions, private sales, and a comprehensive suite of services for collectors and connoisseurs.

Prestigious Auctions

Sotheby’s conducts some of the most prestigious auctions in the world, offering a diverse range of categories including fine art, jewelry, watches, wine, automobiles, and rare books. These auctions attract global attention and often set records for the highest prices achieved.

Expert Appraisal and Valuation

Sotheby’s provides expert appraisal and valuation services for art and luxury items. Their team of specialists, with deep knowledge and experience in their respective fields, ensures accurate assessments and offers invaluable insights to clients looking to buy, sell, or insure their collections.

Private Sales

In addition to public auctions, Sotheby’s offers discreet and personalized private sales services. This allows clients to buy and sell exceptional pieces outside the auction calendar, benefiting from Sotheby’s extensive network and expertise to achieve optimal results.

Curated Exhibitions

Sotheby’s hosts curated exhibitions in its galleries worldwide, showcasing significant works of art and luxury items. These exhibitions provide an opportunity for collectors and the public to view rare and important pieces in an elegant and informative setting.

Global Reach

With offices and salesrooms in major cities around the world, including New York, London, Hong Kong, and Paris, Sotheby’s has a truly global presence. This extensive network enables Sotheby’s to serve a diverse and international clientele, connecting buyers and sellers across continents.

Digital Innovation

Sotheby’s embraces digital innovation to enhance the auction experience. Through online bidding platforms, virtual exhibitions, and digital catalogues, Sotheby’s makes it easier for clients to participate in auctions and access valuable information from anywhere in the world.

What Makes Sotheby’s Stand Out?

Historic Prestige

With a legacy spanning over 275 years, Sotheby’s is synonymous with trust, quality, and prestige in the art and luxury markets

Private Sales Excellence

The personalized private sales service allows clients to transact discreetly and effectively, leveraging Sotheby’s extensive network and expertise
