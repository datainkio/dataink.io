Publix: At Season's Peak

Publix's “At Season’s Peak” campaign educates consumers on maximizing value by purchasing fresh produce at its peak season, promoting a healthy and cost-effective diet.

There's something uniquely satisfying about enjoying fruit at the peak of its season. However, with the convenience of year-round availability at local grocery stores, many consumers may not be aware of when different fruits and vegetables are at their most flavorful.

The Challenge

Supermarket chain Publix sought to educate consumers about seasonal produce and encourage them to buy fruits and vegetables when they are at their best. This initiative aimed to enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty by highlighting the benefits of seasonal produce.

The Solution

Publix launched the "At Season's Peak" program to advocate for and promote seasonal produce. This program included email and text message alerts to notify customers when their favorite produce was at its peak freshness
Bluecadet created AtSeasonsPeak.publix.com, a dedicated website providing detailed information about the optimal times of the year for various fruits and vegetables. The site featured:

  • **Seasonal Produce Information: **Educating consumers about peak seasons for different fruits and veggies.
  • Recipes and Contests: Offering recipes and hosting recipe contests to inspire customers to use fresh, seasonal produce in their cooking.
