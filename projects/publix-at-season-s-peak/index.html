Description

There's something uniquely satisfying about enjoying fruit at the peak of its season. However, with the convenience of year-round availability at local grocery stores, many consumers may not be aware of when different fruits and vegetables are at their most flavorful. ### **The Challenge** Supermarket chain Publix sought to educate consumers about seasonal produce and encourage them to buy fruits and vegetables when they are at their best. This initiative aimed to enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty by highlighting the benefits of seasonal produce. ### **The Solution** **"At Season's Peak" Program:** Publix launched the "At Season's Peak" program to advocate for and promote seasonal produce. This program included email and text message alerts to notify customers when their favorite produce was at its peak freshness. **Dedicated Website:** Publix created AtSeasonsPeak.publix.com, a dedicated website providing detailed information about the optimal times of the year for various fruits and vegetables. The site featured: - **Seasonal Produce Information:** Educating consumers about peak seasons for different fruits and veggies. - **Recipes and Contests:** Offering recipes and hosting recipe contests to inspire customers to use fresh, seasonal produce in their cooking. **Engaging User Experience:** The website provided a deeply cinematic experience, making it easy and enjoyable for users to explore seasonal produce and deepen their relationship with Publix. This immersive approach helped build a stronger connection between the supermarket chain and its customers. **The Result** Publix successfully enhanced customer engagement and education about seasonal produce through the "At Season's Peak" program. The initiative not only promoted the benefits of seasonal fruits and vegetables but also fostered a more meaningful connection with customers, encouraging them to make informed and satisfying choices in their produce purchases.
