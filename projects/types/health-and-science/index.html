Health & Science

I have contributed to various health and science projects by designing and developing platforms that integrate cognitive computing, user-centric design, and data-driven decision-making tools. Notable projects include IBM's CarePlex, where I helped present complex clinical data accessibly and intuitively, and OPENPediatrics, which facilitates global sharing of pediatric care practices. I also worked on the USDA FSIS website redesign, enhancing public health information dissemination through iterative prototyping and human-centered research. Additionally, I contributed to the Enquos health platform, democratizing bioinformatics for consumers and researchers.
