Education

I have contributed to several education-focused projects by designing interactive platforms that make complex information accessible and engaging. Notable projects include the CityForward platform, which enables data-driven decision-making for urban planning, and the Galileo & The Age of Astronomy exhibit, where I created an engaging digital experience to educate visitors about Galileo’s contributions. Additionally, I enhanced visitor engagement at the Institute of Contemporary Art (ICA) Boston through interactive kiosks and developed an educational website for the Museum of the American Revolution, integrating interactive timelines and curator narrations to enrich historical understanding.

If you are looking for a human-experience designer, please do say hello – I look forward to hearing about your company and project.
