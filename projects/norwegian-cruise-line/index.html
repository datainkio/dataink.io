Norwegian Cruise Line

IBM revolutionized NCL.com with a dynamic, personalized user experience, boosting cruise bookings, reducing costs, and enhancing customer satisfaction through innovative search tools and tailored recommendations.

View

Norwegian Cruise Line (NCL) aimed to boost cruise bookings through their web channel to reduce operational costs, expand marketing opportunities, and enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty.

Solution

IBM crafted a groundbreaking user experience for NCL.com focused on the introduction of a powerful and dynamic search interface. This interface simplifies the process of finding and discovering the perfect cruise by allowing users to click on elements they like—such as destinations, activities, or room types—while shopping for cruises.
