Creating a high-touch, luxury online sales experience

IBM revitalized Fabergé's digital presence, creating a luxurious and personalized online experience that mirrors in-store exclusivity and extends the brand's reach to a global audience.

IBM partnered with Fabergé for the relaunch of its brand and website, aiming to establish a digital presence that upholds the prestigious, luxury brand experience desired by high-end market segments.

### **Key Aspects of the Project:**

**Strategic Digital Channel Mix:** The challenge was to create a digital channel mix that reflects Fabergé's luxurious brand image. IBM meticulously crafted a strategy that resonates with the expectations of affluent consumers, ensuring a seamless and sophisticated digital experience.

**In-Depth User Analysis:** IBM conducted a thorough evaluation of user segments and the sales and service processes. This analysis was crucial in understanding the behaviors and preferences of Fabergé’s diverse, global audience, enabling the design of targeted and effective engagement strategies.

**Highly Personalized Experience:** Leveraging insights from user analysis, IBM designed and developed a highly personalized digital experience. This experience integrates high-touch personal sales, phone interactions, and online engagement, mirroring the exclusivity and personalized service of Fabergé’s in-store experience.

**Global Audience Reach:** The comprehensive digital strategy not only preserved the brand’s luxurious essence but also extended its reach to a broad, international audience. This ensured that Fabergé’s prestigious experience is consistently delivered across all touchpoints.
