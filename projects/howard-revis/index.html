Howard-Revis

The Challenge

The firm approached Bluecadet to create a digital web presence that demonstrated its diverse project portfolio, storytelling expertise, innovative use of technology, and commitment to accessibility and inclusion.

A Diverse Portfolio

Howard+Revis has an extensive portfolio of projects spanning various subjects, including history, culture, science, and natural history. Their work includes designing exhibits for museums, visitor centers, historical sites, and educational institutions, demonstrating their versatility and expertise across different domains.

Storytelling Expertise

The design of the site reflected Howard+Revis’s commitment to storytelling. Just as the firm works closely with clients to develop compelling narratives that form the foundation of their exhibits, the site presented case studies as biographies. These meticulously crafted stories immersed visitors in the complete lifecycle of each project, from research and discovery to launch.

Focus on Accessibility and Inclusion

Howard+Revis is dedicated to creating inclusive and accessible experiences for all visitors. The firm incorporates universal design principles and ensures that exhibits are accessible to individuals with disabilities, providing equitable opportunities for learning and engagement.
Even though the primary experience for visitors was mediated through Flash, the content itself was managed externally. This allowed for a secondary, text-based experience tailored to the needs of visitors accessing the site through alternate means.

Innovative Use of Technology

The firm embraces the latest technologies to enhance exhibit experiences. From interactive displays and multimedia installations to augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) applications, Howard+Revis leverages technology to create dynamic and engaging environments.
We developed a custom CMS that gave Howard+Revis content managers complete freedom to tailor the presentation of material to the particulars of each case study.
