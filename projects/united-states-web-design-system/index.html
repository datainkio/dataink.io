United States Web Design System

A Design System for the Federal Government

By leveraging USWDS, teams delivering digital services and products can streamline development processes, enhance user experience, and maintain a unified brand identity across digital platforms. This system is particularly valuable for fostering collaboration and ensuring that digital products meet the needs of all users, including those with disabilities.

The Work

As the design manager for federal projects at Bixal, my contributions to the United States Web Design System (USWDS) included leading the integration of USWDS into various government websites to enhance accessibility and user experience.
I spearheaded the adaptation and customization of USWDS components to meet specific agency needs, ensuring that the designs were both compliant with federal standards and aligned with user-centric principles. Additionally, I facilitated cross-functional collaboration between designers, developers, and stakeholders, fostering a unified approach to design that improved efficiency and consistency across projects.
My efforts significantly contributed to the successful implementation of USWDS, making government digital services more accessible, cohesive, and user-friendly.
