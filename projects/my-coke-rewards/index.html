Coca-Cola’s Largest Customer-Facing Site

My Coke Rewards is Coca-Cola’s premier consumer program, engaging 13 million members with dynamic promotions, targeted offers, and a rich user experience through a continuously evolving web application.

My Coke Rewards (MCR) is Coca-Cola’s premier, dynamic, and intricate consumer-facing program and web application, boasting 13 million registered members. It features a vast network of prestigious business partners, advertisers, and user-profile targeted promotions, continuously evolving to offer an extensive range of features, flows, and offers.

**Strategic Development and Operations:**

Led by a US-based build and operations team, MCR has undergone significant redesigns and continuous expansions. The team efficiently utilizes both onshore and offshore resources to enhance the program's functionality and reach.

**User Engagement and Interaction:**

MCR provides a rich user experience, allowing members to:

- Enter cap codes for rewards points

- Participate in contests and sweepstakes

- Take surveys

- Select rewards based on accumulated points

**Targeted Programs and Promotions:**

The platform hosts ever-changing programs and promotions, in collaboration with Coca-Cola brands and partners like McDonald's and Wal-Mart. These initiatives are designed to target specific consumer segments and drive partner objectives.

**Advanced Consumer Relationship Marketing:**

MCR utilizes a sophisticated consumer relationship marketing database, capturing consumer demographics, purchasing habits, and preferences. This data-driven approach enables highly targeted offers and programs, delivering greater value to consumers.
