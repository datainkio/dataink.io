The Tiffany of Insurance

As a developer on IBM's work redesigning Chubb Personal Lines' website to reflect its luxury brand, I integrated cutting-edge technologies and custom visuals, transforming their online presence and enhancing user experience for clients and agents.

As a globally renowned insurance company, Chubb Personal Lines sought to elevate their online presence to reflect their prestigious brand and commitment to policyholders. The existing website failed to capture the essence of their high-touch service.

The Challenge

In 2000, Chubb aimed to differentiate themselves from typical insurance competitors, aspiring instead to be the “Tiffany of insurance.” This shift in perspective inspired us to look towards luxury brands that epitomize high-touch experiences, leading to a new creative direction.
By 2008, both the internet and Chubb's business objectives had evolved. This prompted a redesign to align their brand and online experience with modern design standards and advanced technologies.

If you are looking for a human-experience designer, please do say hello - I look forward to hearing about your company and project.
