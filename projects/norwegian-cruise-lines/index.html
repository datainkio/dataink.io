Description

Norwegian Cruise Line (NCL) aimed to boost cruise bookings through their web channel to reduce operational costs, expand marketing opportunities, and enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty. ### **Solution** **Innovative User Experience Design:** IBM crafted a groundbreaking user experience for NCL.com, introducing a powerful and dynamic search interface. This interface simplifies the process of finding and discovering the perfect cruise by allowing users to click on elements they like—such as destinations, activities, or room types—while shopping for cruises. **Personalized Results:** At any point, users can view a set of cruise options tailored to their individual tastes and preferences. This personalized approach enhances the shopping experience and helps users find cruises that best match their interests. **Enhanced User Tools:** New tools were integrated into the platform, allowing users to compare, save, and share cruises. These features provide a more interactive and engaging experience, encouraging users to explore and book cruises with greater confidence. ### **Business Benefits** - **Increased Cruise Bookings:** The user-friendly interface and personalized search results aim to drive higher booking rates through the web channel. - **Operational Efficiency:** By boosting web bookings, NCL can reduce operational costs associated with traditional booking methods. - **Expanded Marketing Opportunities:** The dynamic interface and tailored recommendations create new avenues for targeted marketing. - **Improved Customer Satisfaction:** The innovative search tools and personalized user experience foster greater customer satisfaction and loyalty.
