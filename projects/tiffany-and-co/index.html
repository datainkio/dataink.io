Tiffany & Co.

IBM transformed Tiffany & Co.'s digital presence, creating seamless web and mobile shopping experiences that boosted sales, reduced costs, and expanded global reach through targeted marketing and a comprehensive digital strategy.

View

Tiffany & Co. aimed to enhance their digital shopping experience, ensuring users could browse and purchase seamlessly on the web and mobile devices without relying on plug-ins like Flash. Their goals included reducing maintenance costs, streamlining the browsing and checkout process, targeting marketing emails to specific countries, and expanding their online market reach.

Dedicated IBM Team

Our team worked closely with Tiffany & Co. to reinvent their online presence. This collaboration led to a dramatic increase in sales, as the new digital platforms provided an enhanced and engaging shopping experience.

Targeted Marketing and Global Reach

The redesigned digital platforms also allowed Tiffany to send targeted marketing emails to specific countries and expand their online market to more regions, supporting their global growth strategy.

Seamless Multi-Device Experience

With IBM’s help, Tiffany & Co. implemented a strategy that allowed users to browse and purchase using their iPhones and iPads without any code changes, covering a significant portion of the site. This ensured a smooth and consistent user experience across devices.

A Comprehensive Digital Strategy

IBM designed and delivered a suite of 21 websites, kiosks, a mobile app, and an iPad app. These platforms enabled Tiffany to present a luxurious and cohesive shopping environment online, solidifying their status as a premier e-commerce business.
