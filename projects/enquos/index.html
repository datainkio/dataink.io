Enquos

The Client

Enquos is a cutting-edge health and wellness technology company focused on delivering the data-driven insights consumers and caregivers need to develop personalized solutions.
By leveraging advanced analytics in combination with wearable devices, Enquos empowers users to take control of their health and make informed decisions that lead to lasting improvements in their well-being.

Product Strategy

Precision medicine stands to be one of the greatest advances in healthcare since Craig Venter sequenced his own genome in 2007.
As the product designer for Enquos, I had two goals. One was to democratize bioinformatics for consumers. The other was to get as much user-generated data into the hands of researchers as possible. I worked to set the foundation for Enquos to be the tool for understanding the lifelong dialog between genetic potential and the decisions we make.

Evidence-Based Approach

Enquos is committed to an evidence-based approach, grounding their recommendations and insights in the latest scientific research. This ensures that users receive accurate and reliable information to guide their health and wellness decisions.
