    The Minimum Testable Product

    In experience design, a "minimum testable product" (MTP) is the most basic version of a product that can be tested with users to gather feedback. It focuses on validating UX hypotheses and understanding user needs early in development. By prioritizing core functionality and user-centric design, an MTP helps teams refine the product iteratively based on real-world insights.
